QR link scanning added

- QR button in the app header.
- QR button beside the link field in Add Link.
- Only valid http/https links are accepted.
- The scanned URL opens in the normal Add Link form and metadata fetch starts automatically.
- iOS camera usage description is added automatically by scripts/add-ios-share-extension.rb.
- Package: @capacitor-mlkit/barcode-scanning@6.2.0 (Capacitor 6 compatible).
