LinkVault authentication redirect update

What changed:
- Email OTP now passes emailRedirectTo to Supabase.
- Google and Apple OAuth now always use the app redirect URL and open the generated provider URL.
- Native callback remains: linkvaultq8://auth-callback

Required Supabase dashboard setup:
1. Authentication > URL Configuration > Additional Redirect URLs must include:
   linkvaultq8://auth-callback
2. For Google and Apple providers, confirm the provider credentials are enabled in Supabase.
3. In Xcode, confirm the app URL scheme includes linkvaultq8.

If Supabase does not include the custom callback URL, OAuth can still open a Supabase web page instead of returning to the app.
